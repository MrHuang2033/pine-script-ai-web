ICT Silver Bullet Strategy Code V6

The ICT Silver Bullet is one of the most popular mechanical trading strategies. It relies on time-based liquidity sweeps and Fair Value Gaps (FVG) to identify high-probability entries.

Strategy Logic

This script automates the detection of the setup during the specific "Silver Bullet" hours (3 AM - 4 AM, 10 AM - 11 AM, and 2 PM - 3 PM NY time). It identifies:

  • Liquidity sweeps of previous highs/lows.
  • Market Structure Shifts (MSS).
  • Return to Fair Value Gaps (FVG).
